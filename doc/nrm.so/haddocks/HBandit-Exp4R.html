Description

The contextual exponential-weight algorithm for Exploration and Exploitation with Experts and Risk Constraints (EXP4R). See [1]

  • [1] Sun, W., Dey, D. & Kapoor, A.. (2017). Safety-Aware Algorithms for Adversarial Contextual Bandit. Proceedings of the 34th International Conference on Machine Learning, in PMLR 70:3280-3288
